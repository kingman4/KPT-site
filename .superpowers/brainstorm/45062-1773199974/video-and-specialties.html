Scroll Video + Specialties Section

Two questions: how should the video behave, and how should specialties be presented?

Scroll-Synced Video Behavior

The video plays forward as the user scrolls down and rewinds as they scroll back up. It sits between the hero and specialties sections.

A

Sticky Fullscreen

Video fills the viewport and stays fixed while the user scrolls through it. Once the video completes, it releases and the next section scrolls in. Cinematic feel — the user "scrolls through" the video.

B

Inline Block

Video sits as a contained section (e.g., 60-80vh tall) that scrolls with the page. Plays based on scroll position within its visible range. Less dramatic but simpler and more predictable on mobile.
